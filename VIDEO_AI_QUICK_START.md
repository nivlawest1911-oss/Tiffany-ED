1. Click "Avatar Video" tab
2. Enter your script
3. Select avatar and voice
4. Click "Generate Video"
5. Wait for processing

#### Edit a Video
1. Click "Video Editor" tab
2. Upload or paste video URL
3. Enter editing instructions
4. Select style
5. Click "Enhance Video"

#### Generate from Text
1. Click "AI Creator" tab
2. Describe your video
3. Choose settings
4. Click "Create Video"
5. AI generates everything!

## ðŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### Video Generation Fails
- Check API key is valid
- Ensure you have credits/quota
- Try shorter scripts
- Check internet connection

### Slow Processing
- Normal for complex videos
- Can take 2-10 minutes
- Check progress indicator
- Don't close the page

### Quality Issues
- Select "high" quality setting
- Use professional avatars
- Provide clear scripts
- Check aspect ratio matches your needs
