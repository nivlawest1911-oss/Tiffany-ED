**Philosophy:**
The **Rescue One Protocol** is a high-stakes emergency stabilization sequence designed for critical system failures, institutional crises, or extreme operational risks. It prioritizes **Tactical Recovery** and **System Stabilization** over normal decision-making cycles.

## 1. Tactical Recovery Architecture

Unlike standard operation modes, Rescue One bypasses typical risk-balanced deliberations. It is designed to act fast, stabilize rapidly, and protect core institutional integrity.

### **The "Command & Conquer" Response**

* **The Problem:** During a crisis (e.g., massive system outage, legal jeopardy, or infrastructure collapse), normal consensus-building wastes valuable time.
* **The Rescue One Solution:** The **Sovereign OS** transitions into a centralized command mode. It automatically sets risk tolerance to *Extreme*, stakeholder engagement to *Sovereign Command*, and focuses exclusively on one goal: **Restoration.**
* **The Result:** Tactical advice that is direct, unyielding, and focused on immediate survival.

3. **Agent Persona Shift:** The Intelligence Engine deploys "Rescue One" profiles—AI agents optimized for crisis management and recovery logic.

## 3. Decision Generator Integration: Emergency Mode

In the **Decision Generator**, the Rescue One protocol manifests as a specialized workspace:

* **Scenario Type:** Automatically locked to "Emergency System Recovery & Institutional Stabilization."
* **Primary Stakeholder:** "Rescue One Command."
